    Frequently Asked Questioins

    These are our patients most requested questions and answers

    • How do I schedule an appointment with a doctor?

      A: To schedule an appointment, simply go to the "Appointments" page on the platform and select the doctor you wish to see. Then, choose a convenient date and time slot that is available and fill out the required information.

    • How do I cancel an appointment?

      A: To cancel an appointment, go to the "myrendezvous" page on the platform and choose the appointment you wish to cancel. Then, click on the "Cancel" button and follow the prompts to confirm the cancellation.

    • How do I access my medical records?

      A: To consult your medical records,simply click on my profile page, there you will find various information,including your medical records

    • How do I contact my doctor with questions or concerns?

      A: To contact your doctor, go to the "Messages" page of the doctor you wish to contact on the platform and compose a new message to your doctor. You can ask questions or express concerns, and your doctor will respond as soon as possible.

    • How do I pay for my medical services?

      A: For the payment, it is private between you and your doctor,once you visited your doctor you can directly pay them there. An online paymenet feature is in the works and will soon be implemented
